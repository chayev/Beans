About Beans

Beans is a resilient survivor who blossomed after leaving the shelter environment. He's a 5-year-old, 55lb Boxer-Pit mix with a heart of gold. Known as an "A++ cuddler", Beans is incredibly sweet, endlessly curious, and wonderfully goofy.

He's looking for a family who understands the patience needed for a shelter dog to settle in, but the love he gives in return is immeasurable.
